Download Capes and Skins

First head over NameMC (recommended for skins), Laby (recommended for capes) or any other website and download the skin and cape you like to wear.

Move them to Offline Skins folder

Now copy your skin and cape file, and paste them into this path:
.../Android/data/net.kdh.pojavlaunch/[your-folder]/CustomSkinLoader/LocalSkin/[skins or capes]/[your-ign].png

Everything, done!

Open PojavLauncher and run your version with same ign you renamed skin and cape.
